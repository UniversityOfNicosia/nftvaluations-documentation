## Visual Style Guideline
This visual style guideline defines rules and standards that communicate how our brand should be visually represented to the world.
[Jump to the download](#downloads)

## Logo
Our logo is an essential for our brand. It should be used consistently across all communications.

### Brandmark
Our brandmark is our most recognizable brand asset. Use the brandmark on its own only if you do not 
have enough room for the full logo or in cases when the NFT Valuations brand has already been established. 
The brandmark can exist without the wordmark. Diagonal lines are at a 33.69-degree angle.

A minimum area of surrounding space is required across all visual communication. 
The exclusion zone must be relative to the size of the logo and is therefore equal to the width of the 2 column.

![Brandmark](/img/branding/brandmark.jpg)
![Brandmark](/img/branding/brandmark-w_guides.jpg)

### Logo
Our logo includes both the brandmark and the wordmark, typeset in Helvetica Neue LT Pro 83 Heavy Extended and Helvetica Neue LT Pro 55 Roman.

A minimum area of surrounding space is required across all visual communication. The exclusion zone 
must be relative to the size of the logo and is therefore equal to the width of the 3 column.

### Adaptability
The wordmark is used in combination with the brandmark. The wordmark can stand alone, as long as the brandmark is somewhere else in the finished piece.

While the brandmark can exist without the wordmark, the wordmark should never exist without the brandmark.

## Photography
Our photography assets say a lot about our personality and what we are trying to communicate.

Relatable, community, human, confident, clear, concise, focused, aspirational, demonstrates the NFT Valuations personality.
<img
  src={require('/static/img/branding/photography.jpg').default}
  alt="Photography"
/>

Images in printed collateral should be 300dpi in resolution to prevent pixelation. For screen applications, 72dpi is standart.


### What To Avoid
Pixelated or low resolution images. Photography with lacks genuine warmth and humanity. Avoid dark and poorly lit photography. Ensure photography has a clear focal point and captures something meaningful and nonabstract.
