Portfolio management tool helps you group and manage wallets and personalize your experience. Start creating groups by clicking on the purple '+' button in the left column. Once a group is created, use the purple '+' button on the right side to add wallets to it.

![Add groups and wallets](/img/guides/Add-Group-And-Wallet.jpg)

To use portfolio management, you need to:  

1.  Connect your wallet
2.  Create a wallet group
3.  Add wallets to your groups

<Tabs>
<TabItem  value="overview"  label="Overview"  default>
The overview page allows you to monitor generic stats of all the accounts within the selected group as follows:

<ul>
  <li>Valuation of the wallet (NFTi or Adjusted Floor)</li>
  <li>Total balance</li>
  <li>NFTs</li>
  <li>Activities</li>
</ul>

<img
  src={require('/static/img/guides/Portfolio-Management_Overview.jpg').default}
  alt="Portfolio Management Overview"
  style={{marginTop: '5px'}}
/>
</TabItem>
<TabItem  value="analytics"  label="Analytics">
The analytics section displays list of the accounts in the group highlighting their contribution (share) of the total valuation.

<img
  src={require('/static/img/guides/Wallet-Contribution.jpg').default}
  alt="Wallet contribution"
  style={{marginTop: '5px'}}
/>
</TabItem>
<TabItem  value="nfts"  label="NFTs">
See all the NFTs that the wallets within the wallet group hold. You can filter the NFTs by various metrics.

You can filter properties by:

<ul>
  <li>Rarity</li>
  <li>Floor price</li>
  <li>Listing percent for sale</li>
  <li>Last sale</li>
  <li>Estimated Profits and Lose</li>
</ul>
</TabItem>
<TabItem  value="activity"  label="Activity">
Monitor all the activities across wallets within each wallet group. You can filter the activities by event type (sale, transfer and mint).
</TabItem>
<TabItem  value="wallets"  label="Wallets">
This page allows you to manage wallets within your wallet group. You can add, modify or delete the wallets.

<img
  src={require('/static/img/guides/Wallets.jpg').default}
  alt="Portfolio Management Wallets"
  style={{marginTop: '5px'}}
/>
</TabItem>
</Tabs>
